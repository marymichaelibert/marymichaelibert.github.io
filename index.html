Miss Lydia's Coffee Shop

A Neighborhood Cup of Joe

Strangers who bonded over their love for people watching at a coffee shop had a dream to open a place that welcomes all. Mary Michael (Miss) and Lydia opened Miss Lydia's in 2016 and have been serving up fresh cups of joe ever since.

Fall Menu Items

Atmosphere Inside

It's all about the customer. We provide free Wifi for all coffee drinkers throughout the day. We welcome students, business people, or just friends who want to slow down and share a drink.

Rental Space
